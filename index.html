// Who We Are

OUR STORY

Founded with a passion for graphic design and creative expression, Nathan Merch Co. creates unique artwork that transforms everyday apparel into something meaningful and memorable.

We believe that what you wear is an extension of who you are. That's why every design we create starts with a story — a feeling, an idea, a moment — and ends on something you're proud to put on.

Through global print-on-demand platforms, we make it possible for anyone, anywhere to wear original art on high-quality products delivered right to their door.

OUR MISSION

To provide high-quality graphic merchandise that allows customers to showcase their personality, interests, and creativity — without compromise.

// Help Center

FAQ

Print-on-demand (POD) is a fulfillment model where products are only produced after an order is placed. This means no excess inventory, fresher designs, and high-quality production for every item. Your product is printed and shipped directly from a production facility to your door.
We ship to over 50 countries worldwide through our trusted print-on-demand partners. During checkout, you can confirm whether your country is available. Most major regions including North America, Europe, Asia, and Africa are supported.
Each product is made to order using direct-to-garment (DTG) printing or embroidery, depending on the item. We partner with certified print-on-demand facilities that use premium inks and quality-checked blanks. Every order goes through a quality inspection before shipping.
You can reach our support team by emailing nathanandualem57@gmail.com or by using the contact form on our Contact page. We typically respond within 24 hours on business days. For urgent issues, please include your order number in the subject line.
Production typically takes 2–5 business days. Shipping times vary by region: US and Canada usually receive orders within 5–10 business days, while international orders may take 10–20 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.
